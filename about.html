Our Story

We Built This Company Because Critical Infrastructure Deserves Better

R.E.D. Enterprise LLC was founded on the belief that pipeline and railroad operators deserve a contractor partner who shows up certified, works safely, and never cuts corners. Too many managers have been burned by crews who looked good on paper but required constant supervision in the field.

Originally operating as DL Enterprise LLC, the company rebranded to R.E.D. Enterprise to better reflect the mission and values that drive everything we do: Respond. Execute. Dominate. The name changed, but the commitment to safe, compliant, no-excuses work stayed the same — and the same experienced team is behind every project.

With over 30 years of combined experience in ROW maintenance, mechanical excavation, railroad logistics, fencing, and surface preparation, our team understands the technical demands and regulatory stakes of this work. We maintain active ISNetworld and NCMS compliance so you don't have to wonder if your contractor is up to standard.

Based in Anniston, Alabama, we serve utilities, energy, and transportation companies throughout Alabama, Tennessee, Georgia, and surrounding states — with the local presence to mobilize fast when it matters most.
