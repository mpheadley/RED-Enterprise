FAQ

Common Questions

Do you work in confined or restricted access areas?

Yes. Our crews are trained for precision work including hand excavation within tolerance zones and tight ROW corridors where heavy equipment access is limited.

Can you coordinate material delivery for gravel or site restoration?

Yes. Material coordination is available upon request for gravel spreading, access road construction, and site restoration projects.

What is your typical project mobilization time?

For scheduled projects, we typically mobilize within the agreed timeline. For emergency response, we are available 24/7 and can mobilize rapidly due to our Anniston, AL base serving the Southeastern region.

Do you handle both pipeline and railroad work simultaneously?

Yes. R.E.D. Enterprise serves both pipeline operators and railroad companies across the Southeast. Our teams are experienced across both disciplines and can be deployed to multi-asset projects.
